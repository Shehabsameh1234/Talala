About The Project

New Garden City – R5 New Administrative Capital

New Garden City is a large-scale residential development by City Edge Developments located in the R5 District of the New Administrative Capital. The project is part of one of the most important residential zones in the New Capital, designed as a fully integrated community.

The compound is developed as a master-planned community that includes residential buildings, open green spaces, and a wide range of essential services such as commercial areas, educational facilities, healthcare services, and recreational zones.

The design concept of R5 New Garden City is inspired by a blend of classical and modern urban planning, with references to historic Cairo’s Garden City and French-style urban architecture, focusing on wide boulevards, landscaped areas, and pedestrian-friendly environments.
